Since 1984, information regarding domestic assistance programs has been compiled by the General Services Administration, which publishes it in the Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance. As of that edition, the federal government offers a total of 2,574,293 domestic assistance programs. The Department of Human Services by far outpaces other agencies, offering 821 programs, or 72.7% of the total. Other agencies offering a high volume of assistance programs include the Department of the Interior, the Department of Agriculture, the Department of Justice, and the Department of Housing and Urban Development
